The groom

My name is Frederick and it all started sometime in 2015 on Facebook (hahaha). I was waiting for my NYSC in Nigeria while she just rounded up her first degree in the Philippines. I noticed she liked almost all my posts on Facebook and I was very active with lots of engagements from people commenting on my posts. I think I sent her a friend request from there we became friends. She was doing one short course in Business Management while waiting for her graduation ceremony in the Philippines. I used to help her solve a lot of mathematical assignments ( she knows English while I know the sciences haha). We became very good friends. Always talking to each other on the phone (she is a love fanatic, I remember one day we stayed on the phone for a complete 24 hours and even left the call on while we were sleeping hahahah ). One thing that made our friendship grow is the fact that she is the talking type while I am the listening type(wetting concern me kwanu LOL). But before she came to the UK, I assisted her in finding the right school and we did some research together. We started dating in 2016, I had my secondary schoolmate Chris who was also her neighbour in the Philippines then, I enquired him about her, and my guy told me to carry on (na so my mind was made up to carry on haha). When she missed her flight to the UK from the Philippines, I had to leave Enugu (where I was serving) to Lagos Island Etihad Airways Office Church Gate Towers) in 2016, I travelled because her flight was booked from Nigeria and the only way to reschedule her flight was to travel to Lagos. I had to sleep in an uncompleted building ( my friend I went to stay with at his place in the Island, I never knew he was living in an incompleted building. I had to stay with him the days I was in Lagos before going back to Enugu after sorting out her flight issue. what you do for love haha). I later moved to Dubai in 2017 immediately after my NYSC through a work visa. We met for the first time after dating for two years without seeing each other on the 21st of December 2018 in Dubai this was after some attempts to secure a visit visa to visit her in the UK was refused, so she had to visit me instead. We had our first date on the 23rd December 2018 in Dubai Marina and it is not a coincidence that our wedding is also taking place on the 23rd December 2023. Some months after we started dating, I told her that she would be my wife and the mother of my unborn children. We have had our ups and downs, moments of breakups and make-ups. One thing was peculiar, we were both determined to make it work and today God stood between us and the rest is history. Over the years, we both have learned to understand each other and we grew together in maturity. I am grateful to God that we are finally getting married.


Fredrick

Our love story

first meet

sometime in 2015..

The story began some time in December 2015. I went on Facebook and came across a post by my amazing soul (Frederick/Donracy) sitting comfortably pounding fufu. I went to his comment section and I loved how he had replied to everyone on his comment section in a funny way. I was amazed and decided to know him further. I checked his previous posts and pictures and observed he was consistent with his funny ways of response on his comment sections. I was stunned too. I liked his posts without leaving any comment nor chat. Days after I got a friend request and a chat from him. Because I was attracted to his unique ways, I accepted his request and responded to his chats with no hesitation. Our friendship progressed from there. When it was obvious that I was in love with my amazing soul, I made inquiries from my friends who followed him on Facebook. They all said good things about him which gave me assurance that I was in the right direction. I also consulted God in prayers and that gave me a much better feelings with peace within. In 2016 we had moved from friendship to dating. This was the best decision I made because I felt his genuine love in various ways, I was understood beyond my spoken words.

first date

Dec 23, 2018

Above all, our both hearts were zealous for God. We maintained a distance relationship as I was studying in the UK and he had moved from Nigeria to Dubai where he was working. We focused on achieving our career goals while still working on our relationship. On the 21st of December 2018, we saw for the first time in Dubai at the airport where he had picked me up. It was a special day because my relationship felt more real than ever. This time around, I was not seeing or speaking to him on video calls but it was all real. Our first Date was on the 23rd of December 2018. A restaurant in Marina Dubai. It was at night and the sky was so beautiful making him even more handsome. I could not stop admiring my amazing soul. Few days afterwards I returned to the United Kingdom and continued with my studies. It is worth saying that while we experienced much love in the journey of our relationship we also experienced disagreements, break ups and reconciliations. However, we never gave up on ourselves as we kept trusting God for the best in our relationship and consistently communicating and working on our weaknesses and strengths. In October 2020, my amazing soul moved here in the UK. He was in Wales while I was in England. We continued to live apart and pursued our careers.


proposal

November 11, 2022

My engagement was on the 11th of November 2022. It was a surprised engagement. He had informed me that his family would be visiting him in Wales. We were both excited about their coming. I had to travel from England to Wales. I met his eldest brother and wife for the first time where they had lodged. It was a beautiful evening with them and we talked about having a family dinner. On the day of the dinner I got dressed, wore my make up and I told my amazing soul I was ready. He said that he would be going to get some petrol first. After he had left the house, I realised he was taking a long time. I gave him a call and he picked up and told me he was held up in traffic. By the time he got home, he apologised and said he had to drop off his family at the dinner venue. It was quite fishy as we were supposed to go to the venue together. Anyways, I had said okay to his story. We were ready and we drove down to the dinner venue in Cardiff where I met the presence of his family, my family and friends who had travelled from different cities.


engagement

November 11, 2022

At first I was carried away by everyone’s presence, still trying to recognise faces and what the occasion was about. Then I heard voices saying turn around and boom my amazing soul was on his kneel asking one of the braviest questions. Of course I immediately said yes. It was a beautiful day I envisaged. Sincerely, I knew our relationship was going to lead to engagement and marriage but I had no knowledge of the day. Everything felt like a dream but days went by and it became more clear that I was truly engaged to my amazing soul. I am very grateful to God who has brought us this far. With much joy in my heart, I count days to walking down the aisle of forever with my amazing soul.
